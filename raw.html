Our Process

BRIEF

We thrive on the details. A well-prepared brief will give us exactly what we need turn around an equally prepared proposal for your consideration. We recommend asking: what is at the heart of the problem you’re trying to solve? Why are our services well-suited to solve this problem? And what would the ‘perfect’ solution look like?

PROPOSAL

We pride ourselves on the process. Once we understand your needs, we can present the means by which we can fulfill them. Our proposal document will detail; who we are and what we've done, our understanding of the problem and how we propose to solve it, the time we expect it to take and the required investment.

STRATEGIC CONSULTATION

We dive deep. With years building, growing and supporting startups and established brands we know how to get it done. It all starts with a singular strategic direction that provides the catalyst for all subsequent deliverables.

CREATIVE PRESENTATION

We take care. To us the experience of receiving creative is just as important as ensuring its quality. We present our work with the respect it deserves; ensuring you understand its strategic underpinnings and therefore feel empowered to run with it.

DEVELOPMENT AND HANDOVER

We nurture a transition. A creative direction is only as good the way it continues to be implemented. As such we take the time to empower you and your team with exactly what's needed to produce content going forward.
